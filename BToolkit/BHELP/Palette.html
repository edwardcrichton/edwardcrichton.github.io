Palettes

The palettes are available on the TopBar Browse pull-down, and provide templates for machines, refinements and implementations, and lists of the types of Abstract Machine operations available for each of these constructs:
  • Machines
  • Machines Ops
  • Refinements
  • Refinement Ops
  • Implementations
  • Implementation Ops
Templates are also provided for:
  • Bases
  • Documents
  • Enumerations
  • Interfaces
A full on-line help listing is available in the Contents Page
